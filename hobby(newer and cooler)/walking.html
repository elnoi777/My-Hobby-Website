Why I Like Walking

Walking is not just a physical activity; it's an experience. It is how I rgulate my mental and physical wellbeing. Scientifically, Walking is a simple, gentle way to stay healthy. It helps your heart, lifts your mood, strengthens your body and mind, improves sleep, and lowers the risk of diseases like diabetes and heart disease. Even small habits like a 30-minute walk or a few extra steps a day can boost your energy and help you live longer.
